The Avlous way

The name comes from our two eldest daughters, Ava and Lou. What began as Kayleigh's handmade shop has grown into a small family of things we make together, and all of it follows three simple beliefs.

Gentle beats intense

Sustainable habits come from kindness, not punishment. Our tools encourage; they never nag.

Simple beats cluttered

Fewer features, chosen carefully. You should spend your time living, not managing an app.

Made with care

Whether it's code or craft, we make things the slow way, and it shows in the details.
