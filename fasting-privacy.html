Avlous Fasting

Privacy Policy

Effective 12 July 2026

In short: Avlous Fasting does not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal data. Your fasting information remains on your device.

Information we collect

Avlous Fasting does not require an account and does not collect personal information, health information, contact details, location data, usage analytics, or advertising identifiers.

Information stored on your device

The app stores the information needed to provide its features locally on your device, such as your selected fasting plan, fasting times, current fasting status, and notification preferences. This information is not sent to Avlous or to a developer-operated server.

Notifications

If you allow notifications, the app schedules them locally on your device to remind you when it is time to start or finish a fast. Avlous does not receive your notification schedule or activity.

Tracking, analytics, and advertising

The app does not use third-party analytics, advertising services, tracking technologies, or cookies.

Apple services

Apple may process limited information when you download or use an app through its services. That processing is governed by Apple's own privacy policies and is not controlled by Avlous.

Children's privacy

Because the app does not collect personal data, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

If the app's data practices change, this policy will be updated before those changes take effect. The effective date above will show when the policy was last revised.

Contact

If you have a privacy question, email support@avlous.co.uk.
